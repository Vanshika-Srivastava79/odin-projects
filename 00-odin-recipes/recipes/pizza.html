Pizza Recipe!

Description

Pizza is a delicious, savory dish that originated in Italy and has become a global favorite. It typically features a round, baked crust topped with tangy tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of toppings like vegetables, meats, and herbs. Crispy on the outside and soft inside, pizza combines rich flavors and comforting warmth, making it perfect for any occasion—from quick snacks to shared meals with friends.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Mushrooms
  • Olives
  • Corn
  • Tomato slices
  • Chili flakes
  • Chicken or pepperoni (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough: Mix flour, yeast, salt, sugar, warm water, and olive oil. Knead into a soft dough and let it rest for 1-2 hours until it doubles in size.
  2. Make the sauce: Sauté garlic in olive oil, add crushed tomatoes, salt, oregano, and basil. Cook until thick, then cool.
  3. Preheat the oven: Heat it to about 220°C (425°F).
  4. Shape the dough: Roll it into a round base on a floured surface.
  5. Assemble: Spread sauce on the base, add cheese, then toppings. Drizzle a little olive oil.
  6. Bake: Place on a tray and bake for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese melts.
  7. Serve: Slice hot and enjoy!
