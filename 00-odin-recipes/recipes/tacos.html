Taco Recipe!

Description

Tacos are a popular Mexican dish made with soft or crispy tortillas filled with flavorful ingredients. They combine savory fillings like spiced meat, paneer, or beans with fresh toppings such as lettuce, cheese, onions, and salsa. Each bite bursts with a mix of textures — warm tortillas, juicy fillings, and crunchy veggies — making tacos a fun, colorful, and satisfying meal enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (corn or flour)
  • Cooked meat (chicken, beef, or paneer for veg option)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese (grated)
  • Lettuce
  • Salsa or taco sauce
  • Sour cream or mayonnaise
  • Lime
  • Salt and pepper
  • Spices (chili powder, cumin, paprika)

Steps

  1. Prepare the filling: Cook meat or paneer with onions, tomatoes, and spices until flavorful.
  2. Warm the tortillas: Heat them on a pan for a few seconds each side.
  3. Assemble: Place filling in the center, add lettuce, cheese, and sauces.
  4. Garnish: Squeeze lime juice and sprinkle salt or chili flakes.
  5. Serve: Fold the tortilla and enjoy fresh!
