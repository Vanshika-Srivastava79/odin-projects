Pasta Recipe!

Description

Pasta is a classic Italian dish loved for its rich flavors and comforting texture. It’s made from boiled noodles tossed in delicious sauces like tomato, white, or pesto, and often mixed with vegetables, cheese, or meat. Warm, creamy, and flavorful, pasta is the perfect meal for any mood — from a quick lunch to a cozy dinner.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (penne, spaghetti, macaroni, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes or tomato puree
  • Cream or cheese (for white sauce)
  • Oregano or Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • Vegetables (capsicum, corn, mushrooms)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: Cook in salted water until soft, then drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare sauce: Sauté garlic and onions in olive oil, add tomatoes or cream, and season with spices.
  3. Add pasta: Mix boiled pasta into the sauce and toss well.
  4. Garnish: Add cheese, herbs, or chili flakes.
  5. Serve: Enjoy hot and creamy!
